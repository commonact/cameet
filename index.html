About

CommonAct Meet Hackathon, happening in December 2024, is a dynamic event focused on robotics, designed to inspire high school students to collaborate and solve real-world challenges. Organized by CommonAct, this hackathon invites students of all experience levels to dive into hands-on projects, build innovative robotic solutions, and connect with peers who share their passion for technology. Whether you're just starting out or already familiar with robotics, this event is an exciting opportunity to learn, grow, and bring your ideas to life. Join us for a memorable experience where creativity meets technology!
